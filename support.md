About Wave

Wave is a local-first memory exploration app for macOS.
It helps you rediscover files, screenshots, PDFs, notes, and web content through semantic search and visual graph connections.

Wave works entirely on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Wave upload my files to the cloud?

No.
Wave processes and stores all data locally on your Mac.

No files, embeddings, or metadata are sent to external servers.
